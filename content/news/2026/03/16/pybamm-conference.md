The PyBaMM Battery Modelling Conference 2026 will be held in Ann Arbor, Michigan on October 15-16 2026, following the Battery Show in Detroit.
The conference will bring together researchers, industry professionals, and students to discuss the latest developments in battery modelling using PyBaMM.
This year's themes include:
- Physics based models including novel chemistries and degradation mechanisms.
- Numerical methods and improvements in software infrastructure.
- Data driven models and parameter estimation.
- System level models and techno-economic analysis.
- Agentic workflows and integration with other tools.

For more details and registration information, please visit the conference website: [https://pybamm.org/conference/](https://pybamm.org/conference/).
